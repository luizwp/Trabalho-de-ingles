About the Market

Types of Food

Seafood Grilled fish – often served with rice, salad and pirão. Shrimp in garlic and oil – super popular, perfect for snacking on the beach. Grilled lobster – more sophisticated dish, but very common in the region. Moqueca from Ceará – similar to Bahian moqueca, but without palm oil, with fish, shrimp, tomato and coconut milk.

Regional foods Sun-dried meat with manioc (cassava/cassava) – a very typical dish from the Northeast. Baião de Dois – rice with green beans or string beans, mixed with dried meat and curd cheese. Escondidinho of sun-dried meat or shrimp – with cassava or sweet potato puree.

Drinks & desserts Cashew or passion fruit caipirinha – using fresh local fruits. Cocada, cashew jam, and rapadura – traditional desserts from the Northeast.
